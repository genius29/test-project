About Us

The Rainy's Music Store is the ultimate resource for collectors, top professionals, soloists, educators and students seeking all kinds of Musical instruments.

We are unlike any other company because our approach to this business is to honor the perspective of the musician.

Advertising on our site is an easier way to find the right buyer for your instrument or bow. We are not connected to a dealer or shop, instead we function as a gateway between sellers and buyers for some of the finest string instruments and bows in the world, while providing our own expertise in matching players with instruments and bows.

We understand that the process can be overwhelming, whether you are selling your instrument or searching for something new.

Sometimes it is not possible to upgrade to a new instrument without first selling the existing one.

Let Rainy's Music Store provide you with a smooth experience of selling and buying these quality items so that you can feel at ease.

We take pride in our ability to be available to provide assistance and recommendations to you when searching our site.
